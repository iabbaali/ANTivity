ANTivity

"A one-stop shop for on-campus clubs and activities."

Motivation

ANTivity is an event management & discovery platform for UCI students and on-campus organizations. Students struggle to find clubs they are interested in joining and these clubs find it difficult to publicize their events. ANTivity resolves this by enabling clubs to promote themselves and publish their events, while students can discover their interests and register for on-campus activities. Students can save and label events into custom categories and discover events near them that match their availability by inputting their schedule.
